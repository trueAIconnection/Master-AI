## Project Overview

Epic Growth is Malta's premier SME-focused business intelligence consultancy, transitioning from a creative digital agency to an intelligence-driven consultancy serving European SMEs. The company leverages Malta's strategic position as an EU gateway to help businesses expand across European markets through data-driven growth strategies.

### Core Services
- **Intelligence-Enhanced Web Development** (Wix, Next.js, React, WordPress)
- **Data-Driven Digital Marketing** (SEO, PPC with predictive analytics)
- **Business Intelligence Consulting** (SME-focused, actionable insights)
- **Brand Strategy & Design** (Visual intelligence presentation)
- **Community Engagement** (Relationship-driven growth)

### Target Market
- Malta's 35,000+ SMEs across diverse sectors
- Growth-stage businesses with €50k-€500k budgets
- European expansion-focused companies
- Industries: Logistics, hospitality, financial services, tourism, entertainment
